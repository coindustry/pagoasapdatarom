🔒 Why This Structure Protects Investors & Clients

✓

Canadian MSB anchors the structure. FINTRAC registration means Monetary Central Holdings operates under one of the world's most respected AML/CFT frameworks — giving international clients and investors a trusted counterparty.

✓

Venezuela risk is operationally isolated. Local execution sits entirely within Crixto — a separately licensed Venezuelan entity. Monetary's Canadian registration remains clean regardless of local regulatory changes.

✓

Licensed at every layer of the stack. No gray areas — Crixto holds an active, renewed SUNACRIP Casa de Intercambio license (CI-SUNA-2025-02), the official Venezuelan authorization for crypto FX and payment disbursement.

✓

Institutional-grade compliance tech at every entry point. Fireblocks handles custody and crypto receipt. Chainalysis (embedded in Fireblocks) scores every transaction for risk. AML Watcher screens against global sanctions lists in real time — before funds move.

⚖️ Regulatory Risk

Operations structured so international entity (Canada) never directly touches Venezuelan local rails. SUNACRIP compliance managed by Crixto as licensed operator — shielding Monetary from local regulatory exposure.

🛡️ AML / KYC Coverage

Fireblocks for custody, Chainalysis for on-chain risk scoring, and AML Watcher for real-time sanctions screening — layered on top of FINTRAC (Canada) and SUNACRIP/LOCDOFT (Venezuela) regulatory obligations. All funds arrive in crypto and are screened before any disbursement.

🔄 Operator Continuity

Client contracts are with Monetary Central Holdings, not Venezuelan operators. If local conditions require a change in execution partner, clients are unaffected — service continuity is guaranteed at the contract level.

📋 Dispute Resolution

Client contracts governed by international arbitration (CAM Santiago). MKT-Crixto disputes resolved via Caracas Chamber of Commerce arbitration. Both frameworks are binding and enforceable.
